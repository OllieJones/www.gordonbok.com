Timberhead Music is a small publishing company that primarily produces the written and recorded music of Gordon Bok. Over the years, we have also published a few works of other poets and musicians. In addition, we carry all of Gordon's earlier Folk Legacy recordings, plus a sampling of music from friends and associates including all of the Bok, Muir, and Trickett recordings.

Because You Asked

"I keep a very large repertoire alive; I’m always learning or making songs, because they reflect my changing understanding of the world. These are some of the songs you have requested in concert or asked me to record. Here you go, then, and thanks for asking." ~ Gordon

Songs for the Earth
By Rachel Carson (MUSE)
This compilation CD of folk music inspired by Rachel Carson was produced by MUSE (Musicians United to Sustain the Environment). This project features one song from each of 17 different musicians, including cuts by Pete Seeger, Gordon Bok, Tom Paxton, Cindy Kallet and Walkin' Jim Stoltz. This CD is in an eco-friendly package and includes a biography of Rachel Carson, photographs and excerpts from her writings that mirror the songs. View details
